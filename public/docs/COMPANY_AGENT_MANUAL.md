### How Did I Get My Account?

Your account was created by your Insurance Company. Here's how it works:

1. You provide your documents to the Insurance Company:

## Ghana Card Verification

### First-Time Login

The first time you sign in (or if your Ghana Card hasn't been verified yet), you'll need to verify your identity before accessing the dashboard.

### Why Verification?

Ghana Card verification ensures:

- Your identity is confirmed
- You can process payments for customers
- Compliance with insurance regulations

### How to Verify

1. After signing in, you'll see the **Ghana Card Verification** screen
2. Enter your **Ghana Card Number**
   - Format: `GHA-XXXXXXXXX-X`
3. Click **"Verify"**
4. Wait for the verification to complete
5. Once verified, you'll be redirected to your dashboard

### Verification Tips

- âœ… Enter the number exactly as shown on your Ghana Card
- âœ… Include the dashes in the correct places
- âœ… Double-check for typos before clicking verify

### Already Verified?

If you've previously verified your Ghana Card, you'll skip this step and go directly to your dashboard.

### Verification Failed?

| Issue               | Solution                                      |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| Invalid card number | Double-check the number on your physical card |
| Card not found      | Contact your Insurance Company admin          |
| Network error       | Try again in a few moments                    |

## Finding Customer Policies

> **This is your main workflow** - finding customer policies and processing their payments.

### Step-by-Step: Find a Policy

**Step 1: Open Find Policy**

1. Click **"Find Policy"** in the menu
   - Or click **"Find a Customer Policy"** on your dashboard

**Step 2: Enter Policy ID**

1. In the **Policy ID** field, enter the policy number
   - This is a numeric ID (e.g., `163633`)
   - The customer should provide this number
2. Click **"Fetch Policy"**

**Step 3: Review Policy Details**

Once found, you'll see complete policy information:

| Information      | Description                        |
| ---------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Policy Holder    | Customer's name and contact info   |
| Policy Type      | Motor, Fire, Asset, etc.           |
| Coverage Details | What's insured and coverage limits |
| Premium Amount   | Total amount due                   |
| Payment Status   | Paid, Pending, or Partial          |
| Inception Date   | When coverage started              |
| Expiry Date      | When coverage ends                 |

### What Can You Do Next?

From the policy view, you have payment options:

| Button              | What It Does                            |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| **Pay Small Small** | Set up installment payments (financing) |

### Policy Not Found?

If the policy doesn't appear:

| Issue                       | Solution                                     |
| --------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Invalid Policy ID           | Double-check the number with the customer    |
| Policy from another company | You can only find policies from your company |
| Network error               | Wait a moment and try again                  |

### Tips for Finding Policies

âœ… Always confirm the Policy ID with the customer before searching

âœ… Policy IDs are numeric only - no letters or special characters

## Processing Payments

### Payment Options

IPAP offers two ways to pay:

| Option               | Description                     |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| **One-Time Payment** | Pay the full premium at once    |
| **Pay Small Small**  | Split into monthly installments |

### Payment Steps

#### Step 1: Ghana Card Verification

Before processing payment, the customer must be verified:

1. Enter the customer's **Ghana Card Number**
   - Format: `GHA-XXXXXXXXX-X`
2. Click **"Verify"**
3. Wait for verification to complete

**ðŸ’¡ Note:** If the customer was already verified, this step is skipped automatically.

#### Step 2: Loan Calculation (Pay Small Small only)

If using installments:

1. Enter **Initial Deposit** amount
2. Select **Duration** (3, 6, or 12 months)
3. Select **Payment Frequency** (monthly, weekly)
4. Review the calculated monthly payment
5. Click **"Next"**

#### Step 3: Declaration

1. Read the terms and conditions
2. Tick all checkboxes to accept
3. Sign in the signature box (draw with your finger or mouse)
4. Click **"Accept & Continue"**

#### Step 4: Payment Details

1. Select **Payment Method**:

   - MTN Mobile Money
   - Telecel Cash
   - AirtelTigo Cash

2. Enter payment information:

| Field          | Description                      |
| -------------- | -------------------------------- |
| Account Name   | Name on the mobile money account |
| Account Number | Mobile money phone number        |

3. Click **"Pay Now"**

#### Step 5: Verify Payment

1. The customer will receive a **payment prompt** on their phone
2. Customer enters their **PIN** to approve
3. Wait for confirmation
4. You'll see a success message when complete

### Payment Troubleshooting

| Issue              | Solution                            |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------------- |
| No prompt received | Check phone number is correct       |
| Payment timeout    | Try again after a few minutes       |
| Insufficient funds | Customer needs to top up            |
| Wrong PIN entered  | Customer can retry (3 attempts max) |
